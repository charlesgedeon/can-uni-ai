Simon Fraser University

SFU’s “Our Approach to AI” outlines an institution-wide strategy focused on collaboration, agility, and “AI for the Greater Good.” It defines action areas (capacity, classroom use, research, community) and principles like treating AI as tools and staying adaptive.

University of British Columbia

UBC’s GenAI principles focus on mitigating risks: data privacy, security, copyright, plagiarism, bias, and environmental impacts. Emphasizes appropriate use with approvals/PIA where needed, user accountability for outputs, and protecting confidential and personal data.
