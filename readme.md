## Why this project exists

Across Canada, universities are responding to generative AI in very different ways. Some institutions have detailed, instructor-focused guidance. Others rely on student integrity pages, research library guides, or high-level policy statements.

As someone who works at the intersection of **higher education, AI literacy, and educational technology**, Iâ€™ve seen how difficult it can be for instructors and teaching centres to:
- quickly find relevant guidance
- understand how different institutions are framing AI use
- avoid reinventing work that already exists elsewhere

This project grew out of a desire to reduce that friction and support **thoughtful, transparent, and pedagogically grounded** uses of AI in higher education.
